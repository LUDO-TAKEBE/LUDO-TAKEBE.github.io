about


LUDO TAKEBE
V系が特に好き。
1996年生まれ。熊本県出身。九州大学芸術工学部卒業後、情報科学芸術大学院大学(IAMAS)メディア表現研究科修了。
学部では、基礎的な工作や加工についての制作実践から、3DCGアニメーションの制作まで幅広く学び、インタラクションデザインの研究で学位(芸術工学)を取得。一方で、学内有志企画でのインスタレーションや演劇の制作を行い、その中では主に音響やweb、インタラクションシステム開発などを担当。また、在学中にライブハウスで勤務し、音響・照明のエンジニアとしてシステム管理やオペレートの業務に従事した。
大学院入学後は、照明や映像、レーザーなどを用いたビジュアル表現についての数々の実践や制作を行い"リアルタイムな信号生成による照明演出システム"の研究で修士号(メディア表現)を取得。
現在は研究開発で得た知識と現場での実践経験から、舞台作品や映像作品にて音響・照明・映像などの各メディアを複合的に用いた制作活動をフリーランスとして行っている。
株式会社CRYSTALINO 所属。
Born in 1996. After graduated from the School of Design at Kyushu University, he completed the Graduate School of Media Creation at Institute of Advanced Media Arts and Sciences (IAMAS).
At the undergraduate level, he studied a wide range of subjects, from basic crafting and processing practices to 3DCG animation products, and obtain the bachelor's degree (Design) for his research in interaction design. At the same time, he has produced installations and plays as the project member within the university, in which he was mainly in charge of sound, web, and interaction system development. He also worked at the live house while still in school, where he was involved in system management and operations as a sound and lighting engineer.
After entering the graduate school, he has been practicing and producing various visual expressions using lighting, video, laser, etc. He obtained his master's degree (Media Creation) for his research on "A Stage Lighting Control System using Real-Time Lighting Pattern Generation".
He is currently working on a freelance basis, using the knowledge he gained through research and development as well as practical experience in the field to create stage and video productions using a variety of media including sound, lighting, and video.
He is currently affiliated with CRYSTALINO INC.
